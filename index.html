Our Practices Areas

Company Formation

Company formation for individuals, simple recommendation, joint ventures, limited liability companies, stock companies, investment companies, financial companies, and assistance in the licensing procedures for shops and factories.

Family Courts

All cases related to family matters, including alimony, obedience, child custody, visitation rights, termination of guardianship, medical expenses, educational expenses, educational guardianship, proof of parentage, proof of custody, proof of marriage, and documentation of foreign marriages.

Civil, Rental, and Economic Courts

All civil and commercial cases, old and new rental lawsuits, all economic and commercial disputes, bankruptcy cases, economic offenses, trademark registration, patent rights, and purchase contract registration.

International Arbitration

All international and local arbitration cases and all preliminary procedures for international arbitration.

court of Cassation, Administrative Courts, and Supreme Constitutional Court

All administrative courts, appeals against administrative decisions, cancellation lawsuits, education-related cases, health-related cases, cases involving various government ministries, appeals to the Supreme Administrative Court, as well as the Court of Cassation for civil and criminal matters, and constitutional appeals against laws.

Criminal Courts and Misdemeanor Courts

All types of criminal cases, both military and civilian, as well as drug-related cases and state security cases.
